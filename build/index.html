    Bio

    My name's Arjan, I'm a front-end developer and designer currently living in Vancouver. I strive to provide users with well-crafted, simple, and intuitive web experiences that help accomplish goals.

    Having graduated from New Media Design & Web Development in 2014, I know front-end development involves much more than writing JavaScript, CSS, and HTML; it's where all divisions of the digital process come together to tell a story through design, interactivity, and functionality. It's my job to deliver an engaging user-experience, eye-catching design, and code that performs fast and bug free.
